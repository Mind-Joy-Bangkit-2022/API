Best App for Your Mental Health Cheking

This application was made to fulfill our capstone final project. We named this application Mindjoy. Mindjoy is taken from 2 words, namely Mind and Joy, which means happy thoughts. This is in line with the aim of our application, which is to bring happiness to its users by helping them to detect how their mental state is currently. MindJoy consists of several features, namely registration, login, mental health, and expression. The register feature is a feature that is used to register before, and the login feature is a feature that is used after you have an account to be able to use this application.
